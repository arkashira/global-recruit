# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for global-recruit

## Problem Statement

International student recruitment agencies face significant challenges in managing leads, applications, documents, commissions, and student communications across multiple platforms and tools. This leads to inefficiencies, data inconsistencies, and a poor candidate experience. The current manual processes and disparate systems hinder the agencies' ability to scale and provide a high-quality experience for students and partners.

## Target Users

* International student recruitment agencies
* Agency staff (recruiters, admissions officers, administrators)
* Students applying to universities through these agencies
* University partners and admissions teams

## Goals

* Provide a unified platform for international student recruitment agencies to manage leads, applications, documents, commissions, and student communications
* Improve data accuracy and consistency across all stakeholders
* Enhance the candidate experience through streamlined communication and application processes
* Increase agency efficiency and scalability
* Facilitate better collaboration and communication between agencies, universities, and students

## Key Features (Prioritized)

### Must-Haves

1. **Lead Management**: A centralized platform for agencies to manage leads, including contact information, application status, and communication history.
2. **Application Tracking**: A system for tracking student applications, including document uploads, deadlines, and decision-making processes.
3. **Document Management**: A secure and organized storage system for documents related to student applications, including transcripts, certificates, and identification documents.
4. **Commission Management**: A feature for agencies to track and manage commissions, including payment schedules and commission rates.
5. **Student Communication**: A platform for agencies to communicate with students through email, chat, or other channels, with a record of all interactions.

### Should-Haves

1. **Integration with University Systems**: Integration with university admissions systems to streamline application processes and reduce data entry.
2. **Reporting and Analytics**: A reporting and analytics feature to provide insights into agency performance, student demographics, and application trends.
3. **Customization**: A customizable platform to allow agencies to tailor the system to their specific needs and branding.
4. **Mobile Optimization**: A mobile-optimized platform for easy access on-the-go.
5. **Security and Compliance**: A secure platform that meets industry standards for data protection and compliance.

### Nice-to-Haves

1. **AI-Powered Matching**: An AI-powered feature to match students with suitable universities based on their profiles and preferences.
2. **Virtual Events**: A feature for agencies to host virtual events, such as webinars and Q&A sessions, to engage with students and promote universities.
3. **Partnership Management**: A feature for agencies to manage partnerships with universities, including contract management and performance tracking.

## Success Metrics

* Number of agencies using the platform
* Number of students applying through the platform
* Reduction in data entry and administrative tasks for agencies
* Increase in application completion rates and student satisfaction
* Revenue growth for agencies and universities

## Scope

The scope of this project includes the development of the global-recruit platform, including the core features and should-haves listed above. The platform will be built using a scalable and secure architecture, with a user-friendly interface and seamless integration with university systems.

## Out-of-Scope

The following features and functionalities are out-of-scope for this project:

* Development of AI-powered matching algorithms
* Integration with third-party payment gateways
* Development of virtual event features
* Partnership management features
* Customization of the platform beyond the scope of the should-haves listed above

Note: This PRD is a living document and may be updated as the project progresses.

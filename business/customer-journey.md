# customer-journey.md  

**Product:** *global‑recruit* – Unified SaaS platform for international student recruitment agencies (leads, applications, docs, commissions, communications).  

---  

## Overview Funnel (baseline targets)

| Funnel Stage | % of previous stage | Target KPI (12‑mo) |
|--------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| Aware → Consider | 12 % | 1 200 qualified leads |
| Consider → Try   | 25 % | 300 trial sign‑ups |
| Try → Adopt      | 40 % | 120 paying agencies |
| Adopt → Expand   | 30 % | 36 agencies with ≥ 2 additional modules/seat upgrades |

> **Assumptions** – 10 k agencies globally (SAM ≈ 1 bn USD). 0.5 % conversion from awareness to paying (aligned with low‑demand signal, confidence 0.3).  

---  

## 1️⃣ AWARE  

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Trigger Event** | • New immigration policy in a target market (e.g., UK post‑Brexit) forces agencies to consolidate data.<br>• Industry conference “Global Edu Expo” showcases digital transformation.<br>• Peer agency shares success story on LinkedIn (“We reduced document turnaround from 7 days to 1 day”). |
| **Friction Points** | • Over‑saturation of generic CRM/ATS tools – agencies doubt a niche solution adds value.<br>• Limited budget for “experimental” software.<br>• Language/region‑specific compliance concerns (e.g., GDPR, Thai PDPA). |
| **User Emotions** | Curiosity → Skepticism → Hope (if pain is acute). |
| **Opportunities to Delight** | • Targeted micro‑content (2‑min video) that quantifies “time saved per application” (e.g., **30 % faster**).<br>• Free downloadable “International Recruitment Compliance Checklist” (captures email).<br>• Partner endorsement from a recognized education‑consultancy body (e.g., NAFSA). |
| **Metric** | **Impressions → Click‑through Rate (CTR)** on LinkedIn/Facebook ads. Goal: **2 % CTR** on 100 k impressions → 2 k clicks. |

---  

## 2️⃣ CONSIDER  

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Trigger Event** | • Agency clicks a CTA (“See how we cut commission disputes by 40 %”).<br>• Receives a personalized email with a case‑study of a similar‑size agency. |
| **Friction Points** | • Lack of clear ROI calculator.<br>• Concern about data migration from legacy spreadsheets/CRMs.<br>• Uncertainty about multi‑currency commission handling. |
| **User Emotions** | Analytical → Anxiety (risk of switching). |
| **Opportunities to Delight** | • Interactive ROI calculator (inputs: # leads/month, avg commission %, current admin cost). Shows **$12 k annual savings** for a 20‑agent agency.<br>• Live “Data Migration Playbook” PDF (step‑by‑step, 5 min read).<br>• 30‑day “no‑contract pilot” guarantee with a dedicated onboarding specialist. |
| **Metric** | **Qualified‑Lead Rate** (click → demo‑request). Target: **15 %** of clicks → 300 qualified leads. |

---  

## 3️⃣ TRY  

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Trigger Event** | • Agency signs up for a 14‑day sandbox trial (no credit card). |
| **Friction Points** | • Onboarding time: agencies must import leads, set up commission rules.<br>• UI complexity for non‑technical staff.<br>• Limited support during trial (self‑service only). |
| **User Emotions** | Excitement → Frustration (if onboarding stalls). |
| **Opportunities to Delight** | • **Kick‑off Success Call** (15 min) with a product specialist; agenda sent 24 h prior.<br>• Pre‑populated demo data matching the agency’s region (e.g., Thai universities, UK visas).<br>• In‑app “Guided Tour” with progress bar; reward badge on completion (unlock “Early‑Bird Discount”). |
| **Metric** | **Trial Activation Rate** (signed‑up → first login). Target: **80 %** (240/300). |
| **Metric** | **Trial‑to‑Pay Conversion** (within 14 days). Target: **40 %** (96/240). |

---  

## 4️⃣ ADOPT  

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Trigger Event** | • Agency upgrades to a paid plan (monthly or annual). |
| **Friction Points** | • Contract negotiation (terms, data residency).<br>• Payment method integration (regional banks, Stripe, local invoicing).<br>• Need for role‑based permissions for multiple recruiters. |
| **User Emotions** | Relief (solution works) → Anticipation (scaling). |
| **Opportunities to Delight** | • **Onboarding Sprint**: 2‑week plan with milestones (data import, commission workflow, communication templates).<br>• Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM) who conducts a “First‑Month Review” and suggests automation tweaks (e.g., auto‑email reminders).<br>• Early‑adopter “Partner Badge” on the agency’s website (co‑marketing). |
| **Metric** | **Time‑to‑Value (TTV)** – days from payment to first closed student placement using the platform. Target: **≤ 7 days**. |
| **Metric** | **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** after 30 days. Target: **≥ 45**. |

---  

## 5️⃣ EXPAND  

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Trigger Event** | • Agency adds new recruiting offices (e.g., opens a branch in Vietnam) or wants to manage additional services (scholarship tracking, alumni network). |
| **Friction Points** | • Scaling seat licenses can be perceived as costly.<br>• Need for custom integrations (e.g., university ERP, payment gateways). |
| **User Emotions** | Confidence → Ambition (grow business). |
| **Opportunities to Delight** | • **Usage‑Based Discount Tier**: 5 % off after 3 months, 10 % off after 6 months of continuous usage.<br>• **Marketplace of Add‑Ons** (e.g., AI‑driven document verification, multilingual chatbot). Offer a **free 30‑day trial** of any add‑on.<br>• Quarterly Business Review (QBR) with CSM + product roadmap preview; solicit feature requests (co‑creation). |
| **Metric** | **Expansion Revenue Rate** – % of existing customers who purchase ≥ 1 additional module/seat within 12 months. Target: **30 %** (≈ 36 agencies). |
| **Metric** | **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** – projected 3‑year revenue per agency. Target: **≥ $45 k** (assuming $1 500/mo average ARR). |

## Summary of Delight Levers Across the Journey  

| Phase | Delight Lever | Expected Impact |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|
| Aware | Hyper‑targeted compliance checklist + partner endorsement | ↑ CTR by 0.5 pp |
| Consider | ROI calculator + migration playbook | ↑ qualified‑lead rate by 5 pp |
| Try | Guided tour + early‑bird discount badge | ↑ trial activation to 80 %; reduce churn during trial |
| Adopt | Dedicated CSM + 2‑week onboarding sprint | ↓ TTV to ≤ 7 days; NPS ≥ 45 |
| Expand | Usage‑based discounts + add‑on marketplace | ↑ expansion revenue to 30 % of base |
